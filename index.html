About Me

My name is Rashawn Raiford. I amoriginally from Tignall, GA but have resided in Augusta, GA for over 10 years. Ever since I was a teenager, I have been fascinated with computers and taking on challenges. Computers were my way of exploring places I have never been and seeing things I have never seen in person. After high school, I started college at Augusta State University. I graduated with a Bachelor's in Management Information Systems in 2012, but after taking a few programming classes during my junior and senior year I became fascinated with computer programming. After I graduated, I started reading books and doing online courses to teach myself more about programming. After awhile, I became confident in my skills and began searching for a job as a software developer. After searching for months, I finally was hired by a small company called TaxSlayer as a Junior Software Developer.

I have been employed at TaxSlayer for five years now. Complacency has never been my strong suit. I continue to learn new technologies to broaden my skill set. I have earned a Microsoft Certification in C# programming, and I plan on also earning a Microsoft Certification in SQL, along with several others. As the technology industry continuously changes, I would like to be apart of it as it does.
